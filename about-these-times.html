About these Zmanim

Example Calendar

Mincha Gedola

This is the earliest time one can daven Mincha. In case one finds himself caught in a situation where his only option to daven Friday night is at a Tarti Disasri minyan, try davening at Mincha Gedola instead

Early Mincha

This is the pre-Plag Mincha and is the l'chatchilla time to daven Mincha and accept Shabbos. This is custumizable for either 12, 15, or 20 minutes before Plag (Some minyanim say Mizmor Shir exactly at plag. They are the ones who would utilize the 20 minute option for mincha)

Plag

The earliest time to light candles and accept Shabbos. Do not light candles before this time

Candle Lighting

The regular time for lighting candles. As this only works in the US, candle lighting is set for 18 minutes before shkia.

Mincha

Mincha bizman. The regular time to accept Shabbos. This is usually 5 or 10 minutes after Candle Lighting.

Shkia

Sunset. After this time no melacha may be performed

Tzeis

Nightfall on Friday night. This (either 50 or 72 minutes after shkia) is the earliest time one may fulfill his obligation to say night time Krias Shema. Additionally at this time one can be yotze the Shelah brought by the Magen Avraham 267, 3 that one should eat a kzais of bread after Tzeis in order to be mekaiyem three seudos on Shabbos. Some poskim rule that to fulfill this chumra, one need only wait until 40 minutes after shkia.

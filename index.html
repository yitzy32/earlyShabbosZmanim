Introducing the Ultimate Erev Shabbos Zmanim Calendar

We all know what Friday afternoons look like.

This app won't rush your kids throgh their baths but it will
provide you with all the important Erev Shabbos Zmanim.

Many take advantage of the oppurtunity to "make early Shabbos."
But no matter how long the days are in the summer, Erev Shabbos is always busy.
In recent years there has been a movement for accepting Shabbos
at the most optimal early time, which is at Plag Hamincha.
The Halachic advantage of accepting Shabbos at Plag
can be read about in the Mishnah Berurah 267:3.

However locating the time for Plag on your local luach
and then subtracting 15 minutes to calculate the start of mincha,
although seemingly easy, is an extra task on Friday
No one wants an extra task on Friday

That is how this calendar generator was born
Simply enter a zipcode in the searchbox below
and a beautiful printable calendar of
just Fridays, for
just the Daylight Savings Time months, and
just the zmanim you want,
will be generated for your location.

Give it a shot!
